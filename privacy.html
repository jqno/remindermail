ReminderMail by Jan Ouwens

Privacy policy for ReminderMail
This app requests access to your contact list, so the user can choose one to be the recipient of the e-mails that ReminderMail sends. ReminderMail remembers this one contact. It sends mails to this contact, and to this contact only. ReminderMail does nothing with any of the other contacts. ReminderMail only sends mails when the user explicitly requests this.
